Academic Work

Projects

Research and applied projects completed during my Diploma at CPUT.

Mathematical Modelling · 2025

Malaria Transmission Modelling - SAIR Model

Developed a compartmental SAIR (Susceptible–Asymptomatic–Infectious–Recovered) model to investigate endemic malaria transmission dynamics. Derived the basic reproduction number R₀ using the next-generation matrix method; performed equilibrium and stability analysis. Conducted numerical simulations across multiple parameter scenarios to assess how varying transmission and recovery rates influence long-term malaria prevalence, providing insights for public health intervention strategies.

R Numerical Methods ODE Systems Public Health Next-Generation Matrix
Survey Research · 2025(6months)

Youth Agricultural Exodus - Survey Study

Designed and administered a structured survey across two institutions (CPUT and Elsenburg College) to collect primary data on student attitudes toward agriculture. Cleaned survey data using R and Excel; conducted reliability testing (Cronbach's alpha) and descriptive statistical analysis. Key finding: students with rural or farm upbringings showed significantly more positive attitudes toward agricultural careers, providing actionable insights for education and youth recruitment.

R Excel Cronbach's Alpha Survey Design Descriptive Statistics
